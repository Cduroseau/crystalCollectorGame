Infinity Stones Collector

You will be given a random number ar the start of the game.

There are six stones below. By clicking on a stone you will add a specific amount of points to your Avengers' total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each stones is hidden from you until you click on it.

The values of the stones will change, each time a new game starts.
